About this Project

I'm Italian Karsus, @italiankarsus on Twitter, trying to find the artist for every card in the 1991 set, and then in the 1992 and 1993 sets, for completionism's sake.

I've split them into two: a master list of everything, All Cards, one that's got only cards I haven't yet determined who drew, Unfound.

They're mostly new art in the 1993 series.

No further ado here'
