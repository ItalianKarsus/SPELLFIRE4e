About this Project

I'm Italian Karsus, @italiankarsus on Twitter, trying to find the artist for every card put out by TSR!

I've split them into two: a master list of everything, All Cards, one that's got only cards I haven't yet determined who drew, Unfound.

There's also four other such sites, one for the 1991, 1992, 1993, and the 1993 rares+draconomicon+dungeons boosters in Spellfire.
